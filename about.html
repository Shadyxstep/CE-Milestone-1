Our vision

We believe that skateboarding is first and foremost, a community. Our purpose is to connect people through a common passion, skateboarding. We believe that for a lot of skaters, it's difficult to find others perfecting and sharing the same craft unless you live in built up areas. All we want to do, is to make it easier for you to meet other skaters by signing up to our online community. By signing up, we will notify you of other skaters who are a part of our online community and we will act as the intermediary so you can meet new people & practice your skateboarding.
